About the Project: Jiva PMS
Jiva Project Management System


Jiva is a Project Management System (PMS) that helps you manage your projects, tasks, and teams effectively. It is designed to be user-friendly and intuitive, making it easy for you to stay organized and focused on your work.

Jiva is an open-source MT Project Management System that is built using Django, a high-level web framework for Python. It is designed to be flexible and customizable, allowing you to tailor it to your specific needs and requirements.
